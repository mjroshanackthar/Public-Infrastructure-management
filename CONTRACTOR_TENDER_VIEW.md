## How to Use

### 1. Check Verification Status
Look at the top of the Tenders page for your verification status.

### 2. Identify Available Tenders
- **White background** = Available
- **Gray background** = Not available

### 3. Submit Bids
- Click "Submit Bid" on white (available) tenders
- Enter your bid amount (minimum 50 ETH)
- Add estimated duration
- Write your proposal
- Submit!

### 4. View Details
- Click "View Details" on any tender
- See all submitted bids
- Check deadline and requirements
